Hobbies

Violin

Music Performance

I've been playing violin since I was 5 years old. Of course, to me, it's one of the greatest passions of my life. It introduced me to the world of music, and that alone is worth something.

My favorite composer is probably Tchaikovsky or Sarasate. I'm the type who likes those crazy, romantic pieces--all the better if I can show off a little while playing it!

My violin is a 2004 John Sipe, and I currently am having an electric violin made, which was handpainted by my sister.

Gaming

Keyboard Mouse

I'll admit it--I'm one of the 90% of people who like playing video games. It's nothing that special, but I do get a lot of joy from playing, although I don't have a lot of time to play.

Other than that, I do dabble in MMOs and RPGs, and I'm waiting with excitement for Ashes of Creation to come out!

Writing

Fantasy Worlds

I am first and foremost a creative writer; I do my best work when I am inspired to put characters through a wondrous, sometimes horrifying world. So of course, I specialize in fiction and fantasy.

While I prefer to remain anonymous in my writing, I have had my works published before! It's a great point of pride for me, that people have read my work and enjoyed it.

Someone once told me that writing is a form of human connection, that something in me touched something in someone else. I hope that I continue touching people's souls through my writing and my work.
